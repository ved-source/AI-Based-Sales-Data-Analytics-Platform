Model Evaluation & Selection

A comparative review of the candidates evaluated on the holdout test period:

Algorithm Pros Cons Status
ARIMA / SARIMA Handles strong trend variables Fails to parse complex non-linear lag correlations Evaluated
FB Prophet Robust to missing calendar periods Struggles with dynamic interaction variables Evaluated
Ridge Regression Very fast; avoids overfitting limits Struggles with complex multi-month lags Evaluated
RF + HGB Ensemble Captures recursive lag stats and STL trend factors Higher computational requirements Chosen

Selection Rationale: The Ensemble combining Random Forest Regressor and HistGradientBoosting Regressor yielded the lowest MAPE (~15%) and MASE (< 1.0) scores during walk-forward cross-validation. Averaging their predictions significantly mitigates overfitting variance.
